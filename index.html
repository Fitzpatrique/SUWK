Our Solution
By reducing inequalities and creating access to opportunities for people in underserved communities; Skill-Up With Kahdsole is providing Technological innovations and entrepreneurship in the African fashion industry.
  • Reducing the level of unemployment by empowering the African youths.
  • Providing the opportunity for employment for incarcerated prison inmates and underserved communities.
  • Provision of online marketplace for minority groups to showcase their skills by selling their fashion products.
  • Innovating the African fashion space with the use of technology to improve the shopping experience of their customers.
  • Connecting underserved communities with their potential customers whom might be interested in their fashion products.
Achieving Global Impact via Technology in the Fashion Space
Fashion isn't just style, it is a way of life. It is a self-expression and autonomy at a particular period and place and in a specific context, of clothing, footwear, lifestyle, accessories, etc. The sterotypic misconstruction of the African fashion is that the African fashion has been misconstrued as 'tribal' or 'exotic' and simplified to leopard skins and mud cloths. At Skill-Up with Kahdsole we aim at making a global impact with the use of augmented reality in the African fashion space. Augmented reality in the African fashion industry will allow us to build our brand by delivering an immersive experience. Through these environments, we can create spaces that enable customers to engage with the brand uniquely.
